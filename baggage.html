Better Baggage handling with Rapid Analytics

Improve baggage handling by adding details like size, color, type, exterior style, manufacturer etc about the baggage. Gain insights, and improve turn-around time using the baggage analytics.

Faster baggage retrieval, faster turn-around

When a passenger doesn't make it to a flight, his or her baggage needs to be offloaded. Ground personnel have to get into the belly of the beast, and retrieve the baggage, often under time pressure. Finding a bag in the belly without knowing where it is hard, and time consuming leading to flight delays. With advanced details about bags, like color, manufacturer, exterior style, baggage handlers can find the baggage quicker, and reduce the turn-around time of the aircraft.

Avoid departure delays, Early carryon checin

When the cabin space on the aircraft is full, or near-full, the passengers take longer to find a space for their bag. In some cases, these bags need to be collected, and tagged by the ground personnel leading to delays in departures. Real-time carryon bag size, and a digital twin of the cabin space helps to understand the cabin space available in real-time. When the cabin space is full, the passenger's bags can be taken and tagged at the gate, avoiding delays in boarding, and the departure of the flight.

Faster boarding, Depart on time

Historical data of carryon bags on a route helps ground operations personnel allocation to get the aircraft boarded faster. Real-time bags analytics help to know when to start tagging bags at the gate, even before passengers get into the jetbridge, and avoid backing up boarding as passengers look for space for their bags.
