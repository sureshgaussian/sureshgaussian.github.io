Edge, Cloud, AI, and Analytics

We excel in creating Edge Computing solutions that use Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to transform unstructured video into structured data, and use machine learning and analytics to extract actionable insights.

A good solution is a culmination of great technologies.

  • Secure Embedded Systems Edge Device that connects to one or more cameras.
  • Computer Vision Algorithms to deal with the camera.
  • Deep Learning Vision AI to gain insights into the video feed.
  • Scalable IoT Hub architecture on the Cloud.
  • Machine Learning algorithms on the events generated by Edge devices.
  • Data Visualizations to grasp the insights quickly.
