Technology

01

Object Detection & Tracking

The core technology that drives all our solutions is detecting, and tracking the objects in a scene, be it be a person, a bike, a car, a suitcase, a backpack etc. By detecting the objects of interest in a scene, and tracking them, their dwell time, rich insights are derived.

02

Vehicle Counting

Counting vehicles, and their types simplified. Get real-time insights into occupancy of your parking lot, and off-line analytics on when the facility is being used most. Downstream applications include publishing your real-time occupancy to parking aggregators, dynamic pricing, and scheduling maintenance when the facility is least used.

03

License Plate Recognition

Our License Plate Recognition algorithm can detect state, license number, and expiry of the tags with greater accuracy than ever before. With the ability to run on a mobile device, and as a cloud algorithm, we enable automatic gate openers at the parking garages. No more resident, and visitors decals necessary. Simply register the license number as approved, and we do the rest. In addition, we enable smart parking enforcement solutions to monitor wider area faster, searching for stolen vehicles, etc.

04

Baggage Size, and Type

Get fast insights into type, and dimensions of a bag. Enhance baggage check in system with additional information about size, color, and type of material for faster recognition and retrieval of bags. Reduce time to unload bags when passengers miss the flight, and improve turn-around times of aircrafts. Use the technology at the boarding gates to monitor aircraft cabin space, and check in bags that no longer fit in the carryon space. Plus, get rich analytics of bags on a route, and how to optimize operations in that route.

05

Driver License Recognition

Instantly recognize and extract the information on a driver license like the state, license number, address, issue date, expiry date etc. Enable no-touch ID verification and expedite the checkin process. Quickly enable information exchange by taking a picture of the license, and let us do the rest. Get faster self-checkout counters by enabling auto verification of age, and credit card information with the driver license. Other forms of identification are in progress, and we will bring them to you soon.
